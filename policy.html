Policies

Booking

Please contact me directly
for time and dates for your appointment.
Once booked with a non-refundable deposit, I will send you a confirmation text.
If you do not respond to the confirmation text your appointment will be canceled.

Deposit / Payment

A $10 non-refundable deposit is required to book your appointment. This deposit will be applied toward the
total cost of your service. Without a deposit, your appointment will not be scheduled. Methods of payment include:
Cash
Cashapp
Apple Pay
When you submit the $10 deposit you agree to my terms and policies.
If you have any questions about payment, please contact me.

Cancellation / Rescheduling

Life happens and I understand! If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please let me know 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.
There are no late fees of cancellation fees.
Please be mindful of my time!

Expectations

NO EXTRA GUESTS
Please arrive alone to your appointment, unless you have discussed any accommodations needed with me personally.
This helps keep the environment calm and focused for all clients in the shop.
NAIL PREP
Arrive with bare nails, unless your appointment is a Soak-Off or Drill-Off service. This ensures the proper time to give your nails the best care!
DAMAGE / LIFTING
If you experience any damage or lifting within 7 days of your service, let me know and we will get it fixed.
SATISFACTION
Your satisfaction is important to me. If you are not satisfied with your nails, please speak up during your appointment so I can make it right!
